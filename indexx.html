Learn. Teach. Earn Rewards.

SkillSwap SA connects tutors, learners, and peer mentors in under-resourced areas of South Africa. Exchange knowledge, earn credits, and redeem them for airtime, data, and more!

How SkillSwap Works

Learn Anything

Find tutors for subjects you want to learn. From math and science to languages and arts.

Teach Others

Share your knowledge with others and earn credits for every session you teach.

Redeem Rewards

Exchange your earned credits for airtime, data bundles, school supplies, and more.

Get Started in 4 Easy Steps

1

Create Profile

Sign up and create your profile as a learner, tutor, or both!

2

Book Sessions

Find tutors or learners and book sessions based on availability.

3

Earn Credits

Attend or teach sessions to earn SkillSwap credits.

4

Redeem Rewards

Use your credits to get airtime, data, and other rewards.
